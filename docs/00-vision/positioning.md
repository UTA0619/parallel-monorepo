# PARALLEL — Identity OS Positioning

> For ambitious individuals in their 20s–40s who feel overwhelmed by the complexity of modern life,
> PARALLEL is an AI-native memory layer and life-simulation platform that
> continuously learns who you are, simulates the paths you haven't taken,
> and delivers daily, personalized insight to help you live your best possible life.
>
> Unlike journaling apps (Notion, Day One), wellness apps (Headspace, Calm), or general AI assistants (ChatGPT, Gemini),
> PARALLEL builds a persistent, private model of your identity and uses it to run simulations —
> not just to help you reflect, but to actively show you better futures.
